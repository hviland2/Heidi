The Reception Hall

The Reception Hall at Temple Inn & Suites is an elegant venue we provide in every hotel. Our simple, but elegant rustic layout provides the perfect base for decorating to perfectly fit your reception needs and make it an event you will never forget. Our Reception Hall is very flexible to fit your decotating and catering needs. The service providers will set up and take down the decoration settings of your choice; whether you bring your own items or decide to chose from our array of themes and decorations. We will also do all prepping and cleaning up if you chose our vendor for catering.
