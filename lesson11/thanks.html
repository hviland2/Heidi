If you are in danger:

Thunderstorm:

  • Immediately take shelter if you have not already done so
  • Avoid open spaces, iscolated objects, high ground, bodies of water, or any metal objects
  • It is recommended to take shelter in a basement if you have access to one

Flash Flood:

  • Immediately move to higher ground
  • Follow appropriate evacuation signs
  • DO NOT walk, swim, or drive through flood waters.

Hail:

  • Stay indoors, or if you are in a vehicle, stay in your vehicle. If roads become slippery, SLOW DOWN.
  • Stay away from glass. If you cannot get away from glass, turn your back to it and cover yourself if possible.

Hurricane:

  • Stay inside and away from windows
  • Find a safe area in your home such as a closet or bathroom (on the lower lever if possible).

Tornado

  • Go to a basement or a room with no windows
  • Use your arms to protect your head and neck
